## About Me

Hi! I am a master's student at the [Kim Jaechul Graduate School of AI](https://gsai.kaist.ac.kr/) at [KAIST](https://www.kaist.ac.kr/en/), where I am fortunate to be advised by [Chulhee Yun](https://chulheeyun.github.io/).

My research interests lie in the foundations of deep learning, with a focus on understanding what makes modern neural networks efficient and effective in practice. I am particularly interested in how training data and learned parameters shape model behavior, and how they can be better utilized to improve downstream performance.

Recently, I have been working on dataset pruning and model merging. More broadly, I am also interested in the optimization dynamics of language models, especially how pre-training and post-training shape model behavior and downstream adaptability.
